Event Details

All you need to know about the tournament

Join us for the annual city-wide football tournament! This exciting event brings together teams from all over the city to compete for the championship title. Whether you're a player or a fan, there's something for everyone at this action-packed event.

The tournament will feature matches across multiple age groups and skill levels. Don't miss out on the food vendors, entertainment, and community activities between games!

Tournament Schedule

Match timings and locations

  • Group Stage Matches: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Semi-Finals: 5:00 PM - Fields A and B
  • Final Match: 7:00 PM - Main Field
